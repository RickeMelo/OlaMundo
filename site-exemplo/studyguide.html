Please note that the information you provide will be treated confidentially.  Your Challenge Help bring research to life and drive your career forward with the National Research Council of Canada (NRC), Canada's largest research and technology organization.   We are looking for a Cloud Software Solutions Analyst to be a member of our Research Platform Support team. The Analyst would be someone who shares our core values of Integrity, Excellence, Respect and Creativity. This position will be a member of our Research Platform Support team responsible for supporting NRC’s Microsoft M365 environment and managed services (e.g. Gitlab). The incumbent will setup and maintain managed services in Windows and Linux environments, also supporting and administrating M365 products.  The successful candidate will also be responsible for maintaining and completing technical documentation of the services and platforms.
